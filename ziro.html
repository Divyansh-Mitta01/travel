Ziro

Adorning the north east region, Ziro is one of the best attractions of Arunachal Pradesh which is known to be a home to a unique tribal group. Ziro valley is an ideal location for the peace seekers who are looking for an escape from the hustle-bustle. The Apatani tribe resides here and there are pine hills and rice fields which makes this place famous. Alongside the weather here is very welcoming and remains pleasant throughout the year.Attractions In Ziro: Talley Valley Wildlife Sanctuary, Dolo Mando, Tarin Fish Farm Best Time To Visit: May
